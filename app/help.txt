Help

By default without keys passgen.exe generates password with
8-symbols-length and all kinds of simbols.

---
Keys:
-u - remove uppercase letters

-l - remove lowercase letters

-n - remove numbers

-s - remove symbols
---

If you want to use keys, type required length of password:

path\to>passgen.exe 14 -u -s

k4r79w88cbl9k7

---
Maximum length of password: 99 symbols
